Common Issues & Solutions

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page, click "Forgot Password," and follow the instructions to reset your password.

Why is my account locked?

If you've entered incorrect credentials multiple times, your account is temporarily locked. Try again after 15 minutes.

How can I update my billing details?

Log into your account, navigate to "Billing," and update your payment details.

My service is down. What should I do?

Check our service status page. If there are no outages, restart your device and try again.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via email, live chat, or call our helpline.

Do you offer 24/7 support?

Yes! Our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to "Account Settings" and select "Cancel Subscription." Follow the prompts.
