About Kerala

Kerala, located in the southern part of India, is known as "God's Own Country." It is famous for its lush greenery, backwaters, and vibrant festivals. The state is a blend of rich traditions and modern development.

Tourist Attractions

Some of the top places to visit in Kerala include Munnar, Alleppey, Kochi, Wayanad, and Kovalam. Tourists flock to these destinations for breathtaking views, beaches, and houseboat experiences.
